Commute Logistics

According to this article regarding Commute hours, L.A. morning commute hours are generally between 7 to 9 A.M. (inclusive). After assembling a pivot_table for weekdays and hourly counts, I found that 16,887 riders use the bikes during morning hours. Evening commute hours generally range between 3 to 6 P.M (inclusive), and I found that 17,926 riders use the bikes during these times. Although it is difficult to ascertain from this data that ALL riders from these time periods were specifically using the bikes to get to and from work, it makes sense after observing the figure to the left. Approaching morning commute hours, the mean number of riders jumps starkly, perhaps indicating many riders need the bikes for a purpose (commuting?); this suddenly drops after morning hours, and the same pattern follows for evening commute hours. Therefore, since the graphs agrees with human need, I think this interpretation of commuting riders is correct.
